Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our PDF tools

Are my files really private?

Absolutely. Everything happens in your browser using JavaScript. Your PDF files never leave your device — we don't have servers to upload them to even if we wanted to.

Is there a file size limit?

The limit is your device's memory. Most modern devices can handle files up to 100MB+ without issues. For very large files, we recommend splitting them first.

Do I need to install anything?

No. QuickPDF Tools works directly in your web browser. Just open the page, use the tool, and download your result.

What PDF operations can I do?

Currently you can merge multiple PDFs into one and split a PDF to extract specific pages. More tools are coming soon!
